InsightBoard – Product FAQ

Q: What data sources are supported?
A: PostgreSQL, MySQL, BigQuery, Snowflake, CSV uploads, and Google Sheets.

Q: How often do dashboards refresh?
A: Live tiles update every 60 seconds. Scheduled reports can run hourly, daily,
or weekly. API limits may apply for third‑party sources.

Q: Does InsightBoard support user permissions?
A: Yes. Roles include Viewer, Editor, and Admin. Folder‑level permissions and
row‑level filters are available on the Business plan.

Q: Can I export charts?
A: Exports are available as PNG, CSV, and PDF. Email digests can be scheduled.

Q: How is billing handled?
A: Monthly or annual billing via credit card. Invoices are available in the app.
